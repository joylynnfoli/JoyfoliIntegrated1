About



Joylynn Foli CAPRC II, MATS, is an Internal Family Systems (IFS) Practitioner and Recovery Coach trained through the IFS Institute and ICAADA. Joylynn also trained with IFS author Jay Earley for further development. She is passionate about helping others find their inner path to healing.

Internal Family Systems is an evidence-based, powerfully transformative process. It was founded by Dick Schwartz forty years ago and has continued to gain popularity by therapists, coaches, various professionals and individuals interested in self-therapy due to it effectiveness. It can help with addiction, trauma and any myriad of issues that may be obstructing one's perceived growth/progress. IFS can be used to restore balance where internal parts may have taken extreme roles that manifest as eating disorders, depression, addiction, control, anger and anything in between.

The mind is naturally multiple, a perfect tool to deal with the complexity of life. Our inner parts contain valuable qualities and our core Self knows how to naturally heal, become integrated and whole. Using IFS we explore the different parts of ourselves to strengthen the inner wisdom and intuitive guidance residing within that is readily available. In IFS all parts are welcome.
